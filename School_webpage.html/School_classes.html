About Classes



At Mahajan Agrasen Public School, we offer well-structured classes from Nursery to Class 12. Our classrooms are equipped with modern facilities and digital learning tools to make education enjoyable and effective. Each class focuses on academic excellence, creativity, and moral values. Our dedicated teachers guide every student to reach their full potential in a positive and inspiring environment.



Faculties

The faculty of Mahajan Agrasen Public School consists of highly qualified, experienced, and dedicated teachers. They not only focus on academic growth but also guide students in developing discipline, confidence, and moral values. Our teachers create a friendly learning atmosphere where every student feels encouraged and supported. With their continuous efforts, our school maintains high standards of education and overall development
