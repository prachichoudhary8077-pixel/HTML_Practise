About



Reception

The reception area of Mahajan Agrasen Public School is warm, welcoming, and well-organized. It is the first point of contact for visitors, parents, and students. Our friendly staff ensures that all queries are answered and guidance is provided with care. The reception reflects the school’s commitment to professionalism, hospitality, and a positive environment.



Play Ground

Mahajan Agrasen Public School has a large and well-maintained playground where students enjoy various sports and physical activities. The school encourages every child to take part in games like football, cricket, basketball, and athletics. Regular sports events and competitions help students stay active, healthy, and confident. We believe that sports play an important role in building teamwork, discipline, and leadership skills.





Auditorium

The auditorium of Mahajan Agrasen Public School is spacious, well-designed, and fully equipped with modern sound and lighting systems. It is the center for all cultural programs, seminars, annual functions, and stage performances. Students get a platform to showcase their talents and build confidence in front of an audience. The auditorium creates an inspiring atmosphere for creativity, learning, and celebration.



Temple

Mahajan Agrasen Public School has a serene and well-maintained temple within its campus. Students are encouraged to visit for prayers and reflection, fostering spiritual growth and moral values. The temple provides a peaceful environment that inspires respect, discipline, and harmony among students. It reflects the school’s commitment to nurturing not just academic excellence but also ethical and cultural values.
